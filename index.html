Sadhana

Your Modern Mantra Companion

Deepen Your Spiritual Practice

Sadhana is a beautifully simple app designed to help you focus on your mantra meditation without distraction. Keep track of your chants, discover new mantras, and customize your experience to create a peaceful and personal practice space.

Features

  • Mantra Counter: Easily count your mantra repetitions with a simple tap, helping you stay focused and meet your goals.
  • Custom Mantra List: Add, manage, and select from your own personal list of mantras.
  • AI Mantra Discovery (Pro): Feeling curious? Use the power of AI to discover new mantras related to your intentions or interests.
  • Beautiful Themes (Pro): Customize the look and feel of the app with a selection of elegant and calming themes to match your mood.
  • Bilingual Support (Pro): Seamlessly switch between English and Hindi to use the app in the language you're most comfortable with.

Why Choose Sadhana?

In a world full of digital noise, Sadhana provides a quiet corner. Our minimalist design removes clutter, allowing you to concentrate on what truly matters: your practice. Whether you are a beginner or have been chanting for years, Sadhana is the perfect tool to support your spiritual journey.
