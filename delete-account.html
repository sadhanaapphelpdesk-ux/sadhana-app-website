SadhanaApp Account Deletion

Last Updated: August 26, 2025

You can permanently delete your SadhanaApp account and all associated data directly from within the application. This action is irreversible.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the SadhanaApp on your device.
  2. Log in to the account you wish to delete.
  3. Navigate to the "Profile" tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  4. Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  5. For your security, you will be prompted to enter your password to confirm the action.
  6. Confirm the deletion.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon successful deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your user account (email and authentication credentials).
  • Your personal chant statistics (daily counts, streaks, totals).
  • Your saved custom mantras.
  • Your private journal entries.

This action cannot be undone, and your data cannot be recovered once your account is deleted.
