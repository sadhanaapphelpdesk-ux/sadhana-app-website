Privacy Policy for Sadhana

Last Updated: September 15, 2025

Welcome to Sadhana (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). By using Sadhana, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. TYPES OF USERS

You can use Sadhana in three different ways:

  • Guest Users: No sign-in required. All data remains on your device.
  • Free Login Users: Sign in with Firebase Authentication to sync purchases and (where supported) data across devices.
  • Pro Users: Unlock premium features through a one-time purchase (no recurring subscription).

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Guest Users

No personal data is collected. All mantras, sessions, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Free Login Users

When you create an account or sign in using Firebase Authentication, we collect:

  • Your email address (or other login credential if using third-party login such as Google Sign-In)
  • Authentication token to maintain your session

This information is used solely for authentication and to restore your purchases across devices.

Pro Users

When you purchase the Pro version:

  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Payments are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store
  • We use RevenueCat to validate and manage purchase status

User-Created Content

For all users, we store the following data locally on your device:

  • Mantras you manually add
  • Meditation session statistics and streaks
  • Theme and language preferences
  • Custom meditation goals

File Access and Backup

We use your device’s file picker only to import JSON backup files that you select. We do not access your photos, videos, or other media files.

Data From Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication – for account creation and login (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat – to validate and manage purchases (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • Google’s Gemini API – to process AI-powered mantra searches (Pro feature) (Google Privacy Policy)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Authenticate accounts and maintain secure logins
  • Validate and restore your one-time purchase
  • Provide AI-powered mantra suggestions (Pro feature)
  • Track meditation statistics and progress
  • Enable local backups and data export
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Guest data is stored only on your device.

Login user authentication data is securely managed by Firebase.

Purchase validation data is securely managed by RevenueCat and the app stores.

We implement reasonable security measures, but no system is 100% secure. Users are responsible for backing up data regularly.

5. DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information. Data is shared only with trusted third parties (Firebase, RevenueCat, Google Gemini API) to deliver essential app functionality.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access, edit, or delete your meditation data anytime
  • Request account deletion for login users (removes authentication data from Firebase)
  • Export your data using backup features
  • Restore your one-time purchase on any device using the same app store account
  • Residents of the EEA, UK, or California may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA, including requesting data deletion or portability.

7. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Our App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information. If you believe we have data about a child under 13, contact us to remove it.

9. BACKUP RECOMMENDATION & UPDATES

Some updates may change how data is stored. We strongly recommend creating a backup of your mantras and journal entries before updating the app.

We will notify users inside the app if an update could affect locally stored data. We are not responsible for data loss if a user chooses not to back up before updating.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

We are not responsible for data loss caused by device damage, uninstallation, or user actions. Please back up your data regularly if it is important to you.

11. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Your data may be processed and stored by Firebase, RevenueCat, and Google on servers located in various countries. These providers comply with international data protection standards.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be indicated by the “Last Updated” date above. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
